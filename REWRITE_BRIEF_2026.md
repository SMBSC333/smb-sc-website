# SMB Strategy Consultants: Website Rewrite Brief
**Version:** v3.1
**Date:** May 2026

Word-for-word copy and architectural instruction for the website pivot.

---

## 1. MASTER SITE INSTRUCTION

Build the SMB Strategy Consultants website around one front-end offer only:
**Free 20-Min Fit Call**

- Do not present multiple ways to start.
- Do not show public pricing on main marketing pages.
- Do not position the site like a generic AI consulting firm.
- Do not make the buyer choose between diagnostics, assessments, intensives, and programs before speaking with us.

**The Core Strategic Narrative:**
SMB Strategy Consultants helps owner-led B2B service businesses become the most operationally competitive version of themselves by deploying AI the right way. We serve firms that have already tried AI and found it didn't stick — because the real constraint was never the software. We identify the true friction point blocking adoption (workflow, owner dependency, role clarity, team readiness, or economics) and build **Growth-Leveraged AI** systems that deliver measurable margin, capacity, and competitive advantage.

**Definition:** *Growth-Leveraged AI* = AI deployed correctly as a lever for growth, not a tool bolted on top of a broken system. Without the right foundations, AI just amplifies the noise.

### Design Tone
- **Feel:** credible, calm, clear, operational, financially grounded, professional, approachable.
- **Avoid:** flashy, futuristic, overly technical, generic, hype-driven, tool reseller, coaching funnel.

---

## 2. PRIMARY AUDIENCE
Owner-led B2B service firms doing $1M–$5M in annual revenue, usually with 8–30 employees, where the founder is still central to sales, delivery, approvals, or problem-solving, and where AI has already been tried without producing real adoption or measurable operational ROI.
